Reasons to Like Dogs and Fries

Reasons to Like Dogs:

  • Unconditional love and loyalty
  • Great companionship and friendship
  • Playfulness and joy they bring to life
  • Ability to lift spirits and reduce stress
  • Encouragement to exercise and stay active

Reasons to Like Fries:

  • Delicious and satisfying snack or side dish
  • Wide variety of flavors and seasonings
  • Comfort food that brings happiness
  • Perfect accompaniment to burgers and sandwiches
  • Can be enjoyed on their own or shared with friends
