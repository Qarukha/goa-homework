Pasta Recipe

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  5. Add diced tomatoes, basil, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it well and add it to the skillet with the tomato sauce.
  7. Toss the pasta with the sauce until well coated.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves if desired.
